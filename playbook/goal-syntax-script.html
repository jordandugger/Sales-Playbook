# Goal Syntax Script ## Purpose This playbook outlines the goal syntax for creating clear and measurable goals within the organization. ## Goal Setting Principles 1. **Specific**: Goals should be clear and specific. 2. **Measurable**: Goals should have measurable outcomes. 3. **Achievable**: Goals should be realistic and attainable. 4. **Relevant**: Goals should align with broader organizational objectives. 5. **Time-bound**: Goals should have a deadline for completion. ## Examples - **Specific**: Increase sales by 20% in Q2. - **Measurable**: Collect feedback from at least 300 customers. - **Achievable**: Implement two new marketing strategies. - **Relevant**: Enhance online presence to reach new clients. - **Time-bound**: Complete project by the end of the fiscal year. ## Action Plan - Schedule quarterly reviews to assess progress. - Assign responsibilities to team members for each goal. - Document outcomes and learnings at the end of each period.
